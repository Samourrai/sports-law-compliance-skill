# Other Major International Federations

## FIBA — Basketball
**Website**: fiba.basketball
- FIBA General Statutes
- FIBA Internal Regulations (Book 1: Organization, Book 2: Referees, Book 3: Players and Officials)
- FIBA Transfer System (FTS) — mandatory for all international transfers
- FIBA Disciplinary Regulations
- FIBA License required for clubs in international competitions
- **Dispute resolution**: FIBA World Commission of Disciplinary and Ethics → CAS

## World Rugby
**Website**: world.rugby
- World Rugby Laws of the Game
- World Rugby Regulations (27 regulations covering eligibility, anti-doping, agents, etc.)
- **Eligibility**: Nationality-based; Regulation 8 governs international eligibility
- **Player transfers**: No formal TMS equivalent; governed by contract law + national union rules
- **Dispute resolution**: Judicial Committee → Appeal Committee → CAS

## World Athletics (formerly IAAF)
**Website**: worldathletics.org
- World Athletics Constitution
- World Athletics Competition Rules
- World Athletics Anti-Doping Rules (aligned with WADC)
- Eligibility rules including nationality transfer rules (3-year waiting period)
- **Dispute resolution**: Athletics Integrity Unit (AIU) → CAS

## ITF / ATP / WTA — Tennis
- **ITF**: itftennis.com — Davis Cup, Fed Cup, Olympic tennis
- **ATP**: atptour.com — Men's professional circuit
- **WTA**: wtatennis.com — Women's professional circuit
- Anti-Doping: ITF Tennis Anti-Doping Programme (TADP)
- Integrity: Tennis Integrity Unit (TIU) — match-fixing
- **Dispute resolution**: Internal tribunal → CAS

## ICC — Cricket
**Website**: icc-cricket.com
- ICC Code of Conduct — match conduct violations
- ICC Anti-Corruption Code
- ICC Anti-Doping Code
- **Dispute resolution**: Internal → CAS

## FIVB — Volleyball
**Website**: fivb.com
- FIVB Statutes and Regulations
- FIVB Transfer Regulations
- FIVB Anti-Doping Regulations (WADC compliant)

## UEFA Champions League / Competition-Specific
For clubs in UEFA competitions:
- Load `football-bodies.md` for UEFA-specific rules
- Key document: UEFA Club Competitions Regulations (updated each season)
- Protest procedure: Submit to UEFA match delegate immediately or within **24 hours** of match

## General Principles Across All Federations
1. **Exhaust internal remedies** before going to CAS
2. **Time limits are strict** — missing a deadline often means losing the right to appeal
3. **Written form matters** — most protests and appeals must be in writing
4. **Documentation**: Always preserve evidence (videos, contracts, medical records, communications)
5. **Agent regulations**: Most IFs now regulate agents/intermediaries — check registration requirements
